# Restormel Homepage Wireframe and Copy Deck

## Purpose
Define the homepage structure and core copy for `restormel.dev`.

## Homepage goal
Move a new visitor from curiosity to action in under two minutes.

Primary action:
**Open Workspace Demo**

Secondary action:
**Read Docs**

## Messaging hierarchy
1. Restormel is a graph-native reasoning debugger and evaluator.
2. It compiles traces, retrieval, and evidence into structured reasoning objects.
3. It helps teams inspect support, contradictions, provenance, and run-to-run changes.
4. It integrates with existing systems rather than replacing them.

## Wireframe

### Section 1 — Hero
**Headline:**  
Debug AI reasoning, not just logs.

**Subhead:**  
Restormel turns traces, retrieval events, and evidence into structured reasoning objects you can inspect, compare, and audit.

**CTA 1:** Open Workspace Demo  
**CTA 2:** Read Docs

### Section 2 — Why this matters
Short explainer:
Teams can already inspect prompts, traces, and retrieved documents. They still struggle to see which claims were supported, where support was weak, what contradicted what, and what changed between runs. Restormel solves that layer.

### Section 3 — What you can inspect
Cards:
- Support
- Contradiction
- Provenance
- Compare runs
- Evidence state
- Lineage export

### Section 4 — How it works
1. Import or receive run data.
2. Compile to a reasoning object.
3. Inspect graph, evidence, and provenance.
4. Compare and evaluate.
5. Export lineage or share with the team.

### Section 5 — Product strip
- Reasoning Workspace
- Graph-aware Evals
- Lineage Exports

### Section 6 — Integrates with your stack
Copy:
Restormel sits above your existing tracing, retrieval, and model stack. It does not ask you to replace them.

### Section 7 — SOPHIA proof point
Copy:
SOPHIA is the first downstream application built on Restormel’s core contracts and reasoning workflows.

### Section 8 — Closing CTA
**See why the system answered that way.**
Open the demo or bring your own run.

## Copy rules
- never lead with “platform” language
- lead with concrete inspection value
- keep GraphRAG subordinate to diagnostics language
- keep the tone calm and technical
