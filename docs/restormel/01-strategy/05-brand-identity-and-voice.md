# Restormel Brand Identity and Voice

## Purpose
Define the brand identity, positioning, and voice for Restormel so that all external materials describe the same company, platform, and product family.

## Brand role in the ecosystem

### Parent company
**Allotment Technology Ltd**
The legal and strategic parent company.

### Platform brand
**Restormel**
The platform brand for graph-native reasoning debugging, evaluation, and lineage.

### Reference application
**SOPHIA**
The flagship downstream application and public proof of the platform.

## Brand idea
Restormel makes reasoning visible.

Not in a decorative sense. In a practical engineering sense:
- visible enough to debug
- visible enough to compare
- visible enough to evaluate
- visible enough to govern

## Positioning statement
Restormel is a graph-native reasoning debugger and evaluator for AI systems. It compiles traces, retrieval events, and evidence into structured reasoning objects that teams can inspect, compare, and audit.

## Tone
- calm
- technical
- premium
- precise
- unsensational
- commercially literate

## What the voice should avoid
- vague “future of intelligence” language
- generic platform clichés
- sounding like a generic observability vendor
- sounding like a generic RAG framework
- overclaiming formal certainty where the system provides structured inspection

## Messaging rules
- say “reasoning workspace” more often than “graph viewer”
- say “reasoning object” when referring to the canonical compiled structure
- say “integrates with your existing traces and retrieval stack” rather than “replace your stack”
- describe SOPHIA as the first downstream consumer, not the platform itself

## Visual direction
- dark, calm, technical
- semantic clarity over flourish
- graph views that feel analytical rather than ornamental
- provenance, contradiction, and support made legible
- restrained motion

## Core phrases
Use:
- graph-native reasoning debugger and evaluator
- reasoning workspace
- canonical reasoning objects
- graph-aware diagnostics
- support, contradiction, provenance, lineage
- compare runs, graph states, and evidence states

Avoid:
- all-in-one AI platform
- end-to-end RAG stack
- universal agent infrastructure
- next-generation observability platform
