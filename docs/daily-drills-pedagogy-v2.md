## What We Took Into SOPHIA
1. Explicit guided path
- Learners see an always-visible progression map from Orientation to Degree-Level Writer.
- Stage transitions are tied to completed lessons, essay reviews, and critique depth.

2. Short active drill loop
- Daily drills now run in three steps:
- Step 1: interactive concept check (immediate correctness feedback),
- Step 2: 100-word reasoning sprint,
- Step 3: saved reflection/feedback completion.

3. Mastery before escalation
- Step 2 is locked until Step 1 quiz is passed.
- Writing submission is bounded to concise short-answer limits for fast formative feedback.

4. Immediate, formative coaching
- Daily sprint uses SOPHIA’s analysis/critique/synthesis mini review.
- Learner gets actionable feedback in-session, not delayed batch scoring.

## Pedagogical Intent
- Keep novices engaged via quick wins and visible forward movement.
- Build retrieval habits before long-form writing.
- Progress from concept recognition to argument production to essay synthesis.
